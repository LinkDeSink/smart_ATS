    What is an ATS and why does it matter?

    ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Most companies use them to filter out resumes before a human ever sees them. Smart ATS helps you optimize your resume specifically for these algorithms.

  • help

    How does the AI analysis work?

    We use Google Gemini AI to cross-reference your CV with specific job descriptions. Our AI identifies missing keywords, formatting issues, and provides a matching score. We provide a whole lot of other features, but you have to sign up to find out.

  • help

    Is my data secure?

    Yes. Smart ATS uses industry-standard encryption. Your CVs and job search data are private and never shared with third parties.

  • help

    Does it support PDF and DOCX formats?

    Absolutely. You can upload your CV in PDF or DOCX format, and our system will extract and analyze the content securely and accurately.
